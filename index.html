Who Am I

Isaac Woo Personal Trainer Rehab Specialist

Hi, I’m Isaac, a neuro-trained rehabilitation therapist and personal trainer specializing in helping people move better, feel stronger, and take control of their own recovery. My approach goes beyond traditional rehab and fitness—I integrate functional neurology to optimize movement, reduce pain, and improve performance. Whether you're recovering from an injury, dealing with chronic pain, or striving for peak physical function, I focus on education and self-sufficiency, so you’re not dependent on therapy forever.
